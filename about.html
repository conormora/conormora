About Me

Hi there! I am currently a junior at the University of California, Berkeley majoring in Computer Science (B.A.) and Business Administration (B.S.). I am interested in the intersection of technology and business; I aim to consult on the world's most pressing issues, and create organizations and products to solve them.

During my past two years at UC Berkeley, I've been an Education Consultant, Event Planner, and Robotics Mentor for Pioneers in Engineering, co-taught the 80-person PiE Robotics DeCal, and am currently the External Vice President of ANova, a CS mentorship organization for under-resourced youth in the East Bay. I am also working as a COVID-19 Task Force Researcher where I am creating an epidemiological model (SAIR) to represent the disease dynamics of COVID-19. At UC Berkeley, I am currently co-founding a public policy think-tank.

I have previously interned at URx/InternHacks (Software Engineering Intern, chosen out of 800+ applicants), Franklin Templeton Investments (Fintech Partnerships & Corporate Venture Capital Intern), Infima (Data Science Researcher Intern), Propel(x) (Product Marketing Consultant Intern), and GoodlyLabs (Software Development Intern). I am a Jacobs Institute Innovation Catalysts Grant Recipient (1 of the 8 Fall 2019 recipients) and have won the Best Future Vision Award in the FinTech division for my android app at InternHacks.
